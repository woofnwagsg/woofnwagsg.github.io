The Woof & Wag Difference

About Us

At Woof & Wag, we believe every dog deserves to be understood — not just managed. Founded by Andy, an experienced dog trainer with a passion for structure, trust, and lasting results, Woof & Wag was built on one simple belief: when dogs thrive, families thrive.

We work with all dog owners — from first-timers navigating puppyhood to seasoned owners dealing with complex behaviours. What sets us apart isn't just our methods — it's how deeply we get to know each dog before we ever begin. Every programme, every interaction, and every recommendation is tailored specifically to your dog.

At Woof & Wag, you won't find a one-size-fits-all approach. You'll find a team that's honest, knowledgeable, and genuinely invested in your dog's progress.

Meet Your Trainer

About Andy

Before I became a dog trainer, I served in Singapore's K9 Unit during National Service — working hands-on with dogs in one of the most demanding environments possible. That experience didn't just teach me how dogs behave. It taught me how to understand them.

After years of working with dogs at that level, I knew I wanted to bring that same discipline and expertise to everyday dog owners — especially those who had tried everything and still couldn't find answers. That's why I started Woof & Wag.

What we do here is different. We don't just supervise your dog. We study them, understand them, and put them through structured, complete training programmes — not the kind that stops halfway and leaves you guessing. Every owner walks away knowing exactly what to do, how to do it, and what to look out for.

Our focus is simple: build your dog's confidence, shape their manners, and give you the tools to maintain that progress long after they leave us.

Because a well-understood dog isn't just better behaved — they're happier.

Expert Guidance

Dog Training

Good behaviour isn't taught by chance — it's built through the right foundation.

At Woof & Wag, our training programmes draw from positive reinforcement, balanced training, and science-based methods, guided by structured pack leadership principles. This combination allows us to meet each dog where they are and bring out the best in them — regardless of breed, age, or background.

Before any training begins, our trainer takes the time to assess your dog's temperament, history, and specific needs. From there, a fully customised programme is designed — with clear goals, transparent progress updates, and practical guidance you can carry forward at home.

Whether you're working on basic obedience, recall, leash manners, or more complex behavioural challenges, we give you the tools and understanding to build a relationship with your dog that lasts.

Premium Care

Dog Daycare

More than just a place to spend the day — a structured environment where dogs genuinely flourish.

Woof & Wag's daycare is designed with your dog's wellbeing and development in mind. Every day includes supervised free play, structured activities, and meaningful socialisation — all tailored to suit each dog's personality and comfort level.

We keep our groups intentionally small, ensuring every dog receives the individual attention they deserve. Andy and the team closely monitor behaviour, energy levels, and interactions throughout the day, so you can drop off with confidence and pick up a happy, well-balanced dog.

Daycare at Woof & Wag isn't babysitting — it's an extension of the structure and care your dog gets at home.
